You can find lot&nbsp;of IT related stuff - interviews, articles, presentations slides and videos. And today I would like to talk about videos from InfoQ in particular. Is always good to have offline version to watch it during long commute, for instance.&nbsp;Unfortunately, InfoQ administration for some reasons not allow to download videos. 
However, I found very elegant solution how to do that. I did it on my MacOS X with Safari, probably it will work with Safari for Windows as well.
<a name='more'></a>
